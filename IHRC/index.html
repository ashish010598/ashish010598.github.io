Our Mission
The mission of the Indian Human Rights Commission (IHRC) is to promote, protect, and uphold the fundamental rights and dignity of every individual across India. Committed to the principles enshrined in the Universal Declaration of Human Rights, IHRC works tirelessly to combat discrimination, injustice, and violence in all forms. Through advocacy, education, and collaboration with stakeholders, we strive to ensure equal rights, social justice, and the rule of law for all members of society, particularly marginalized and vulnerable communities. By fostering a culture of accountability and transparency, IHRC endeavors to create a nation where human rights are respected, protected, and realized for every citizen.

Monitoring and Investigation
The IHRC conducts in-depth investigations into allegations of human rights abuses across India, particularly in cases involving marginalized communities such as Dalits, women, children, religious minorities, and persons with disabilities.
