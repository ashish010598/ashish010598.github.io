What We Do

Indian Human Right Commission

Human Rights

Human rights are moral principles or norms for certain standards of human behaviour and are regularly protected in municipal and international law.

Child Rights

Child rights are the human rights of children with particular attention to the rights of special protection and care afforded to minors

Labour Rights

Labour rights are the legal relationship between workers and employers, usually dealt with employment law.

Women Rights

women rights are the rights and entitlements claimed, usually dealt with women and domestic crime

Freedom of speech

Freedom of speech is a principle that supports the freedom of an individual or a community to articulate their opinions and ideas without fear of retaliation, censorship, or legal sanction.
