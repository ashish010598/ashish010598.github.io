About Us

At the Indian Human Rights Commission (IHRC), our mission is to safeguard and promote the fundamental rights and dignity of every individual across India. Guided by the principles of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights, we are dedicated to combating discrimination, injustice, and violence in all forms.
Through advocacy, education, and partnerships with diverse stakeholders, IHRC works to uphold equality, social justice, and the rule of law, with a special focus on protecting the rights of marginalized and vulnerable communities. Our commitment to accountability and transparency drives us to foster a culture where human rights are not only respected and protected but also fully realized for every citizen.
Together, we aim to build a just and inclusive society where every individual can thrive with dignity and equality.

Dignity and Equality

We believe every individual deserves respect and equal treatment, regardless of their background, identity, or circumstances.

Justice and Fairness

We are committed to ensuring social justice and the rule of law, advocating for fairness and accountability in all spheres of society.

Inclusivity and Empowerment

We prioritize the voices of marginalized and vulnerable communities, working to empower them and promote their rights.

Integrity and Transparency

We uphold the highest standards of accountability and openness, fostering trust and credibility in all our actions and initiatives.
